Surface Temperature Data Visualization

This data was provided by the Food and Agriculture Organization Corporate Statistical Database
and is based on the publically avaliable GISTEMP data from the National Aeronautics and Space Administration Goddard Institute for Space Studies.
Please note that the dataset isn't fully complete, there are sections of missing data for certain years for certain countries.

My goal is simply to make this information on climate change a little bit more accessible, I hope that anyone that comes across this will find it useful, thank you :]
